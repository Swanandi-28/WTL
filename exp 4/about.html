Indian Cuisine Gallery

About Us

We showcase the rich diversity of Indian cuisine through a modern and responsive web gallery. This project is designed using Bootstrap and JavaScript to provide a smooth and interactive user experience.

Indian cuisine is known for its variety of flavors, spices, and cooking techniques. Our gallery highlights famous dishes from different regions including North Indian, South Indian, and street food culture.

🍛 Variety of Dishes

Explore dishes like biryani, dosa, paneer, and traditional Indian thali.

📱 Responsive Design

Our gallery is fully responsive and works on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

⚡ Interactive UI

Includes hover effects and modal view using JavaScript for better interaction.

🎯 Our Mission

To present Indian cuisine in a visually appealing and interactive digital format.

🚀 Our Vision

To create modern web applications that combine creativity, design, and functionality.
